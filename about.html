Hello! I'm Leura Smith, an indie game developer, based in Melbourne, Australia. I aim to make games anyone can play, especially women and children.

I've worked on a pretty broad range of projects, from games about pigeons, to more arty, exhibition games, to games made with architecture studios. I really love making games!

I've done some talks about my design process -

  • IndieCade 2019 - 'The importance of kindness'
  • ACMI, 2019, ACMI EDU - A conversation with Leura Smith
  • Freeplay Parallels, 2018, Melbourne International Games Week - Designing a game for my mum (Pigeon Game)
