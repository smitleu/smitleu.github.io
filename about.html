About me :)

Hello! I'm Leura Smith, an indie game developer, based in Melbourne, Australia. I'm a keen game designer and graduate architect, and I love all things about... well... those two things! .

I've worked on a pretty broad range of game projects, from games about pigeons, to more arty, exhibition games, to games made with architecture studios. I really love making games!

I've done some cool stuff with architecture too - one is my submission for a school at the Seoul Bienalle, in 2023.

I've done some talks about my design process -

  • IndieCade 2019 - 'The importance of kindness'
  • ACMI, 2019, ACMI EDU - A conversation with Leura Smith
  • Freeplay Parallels, 2018, Melbourne International Games Week - Designing a game for my mum (Pigeon Game)
