Pigeon Game

Pigeon Game is a game about playing with... pigeons! In Pigeon Game, you can chase, feed, pet, befriend and hold pigeons. I made this game primarily as a thing for women and children to enjoy.

- IndieCade 2019 USA, Kindness in Games
- Freeplay Parallels 2018, Making a Game for my Mum (Pigeon Game) (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=edEWV0UNpx4&t=1084s)

Pigeon Game has also shown at a few exhibitions:
- GCAP 2019, Hovergarden 'cozy space' game feature
- 'Froth women in games' 2019 Bar SK Exhibition
- ACMI Audience Lab 2018 game feature

It's also won an award, nice!

Dune Creatures

Dune Creatures is a game I made in collaboration with BlacketSmith Architects, and is about exploring spaces that many people will never see. It's based off a model of their 'North Coogee Picnic Shelters' - you can read about the physical structure here . I wanted to play with bringing physical places onto the virtual plane, and I had a great time doing it.

DelEAT

DelEAT was a game I was commissioned to make for GDC Delete 2019.

The premise of Delete was that all the featured games would be deleted at midnight. It was for GDC and it was fun to be comissioned to a game for!

This means that delEAT no longer exists. It was a game about making cakes, based off the Cooking Mama and Tamagotchi games (Nintendo DS). Players made cakes and put them in the oven to cook. At midnight, all the cakes finished cooking, and exploded out of the oven and onto the screen.

It was great seeing everyone cheer and get excited about making cakes, and weirdly therapeutic deleting the game in the middle of the party. We gave everyone sweets when the game got deleted, so it was like their cakes came out of the game, and into the real life party!

I made Find The Plot in a week, as part of a 'Game a Week' exercise.
